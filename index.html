About

I wanted to start an online compilation of new and old writing (mostly poetry, sometimes an informal online diary entry) to share with whoever stumbles upon this page. Words have changed my life, the way I see myself, the way I see the world, and everything in between. I have always been a bit scared to show vulnerability especially through writing, so I wanted to start somewhere, and that place is here!
