about me

Writing is the only thing that I truly feel like is mine. It has comforted me and confronted me in trying times. I have learned a lot about myself through words - whether it is through my own or another person's. I believe that being able to read people's writing, in all forms, is one of the most intimate ways to get to know someone. I was never too good at intimacy. This is one of my attempts at getting better. 

here's to more lessons.
